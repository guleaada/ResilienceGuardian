🌿 SebilAI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Developer: Gulilat Kasiye Worku · gulilatkasiye4@gmail.com

🌍 This policy is also available inside the app in 6 languages: English · አማርኛ · Oromiffa · ትግርኛ · Soomaali · Qafar

Summary — Simple Version

Your data stays on your phone. We do not sell, share, or track farmer data. The app works fully offline. When you do use AI analysis, only your crop photo and symptoms are sent — never your name or phone number.

2. What We NEVER Collect

❌ No user accounts ❌ No passwords ❌ No GPS coordinates stored ❌ No advertising tracking ❌ No cookies ❌ No analytics ❌ No data sold ❌ Name/phone never sent to AI

3. AI Analysis Data Flow

When you tap "Analyze with AI":

  1. Your photo and selected symptoms go to our server (Render.com, USA)
  2. Server sends to AI provider (Groq or Google Gemini) — without your name, phone, or location
  3. AI returns diagnosis text — we forward it to your phone
  4. Nothing is stored on the server

AI providers used: Groq Privacy Policy · Google Gemini Terms

4. SMS (Africa's Talking)

SMS is only sent when you explicitly tap "Send SMS" and enter a phone number. We use Africa's Talking (Kenya/Ethiopia). See Africa's Talking Privacy Policy. Phone numbers are never stored on our server.

5. Data Deletion

All your data is stored on your device. To delete everything:

  • On phone browser: Settings → Clear Site Data for sebilai.com
  • Diagnosis history: Tap History → Delete All
  • Profile: Clear fields in Profile tab and save
