# 🌿 Resilience Guardian

**AI-powered crop disease advisor for Ethiopian smallholder farmers**

Built by **Gulilat Kasiye** — Ethiopian AI Developer

---

## What It Does

Resilience Guardian helps Ethiopian farmers diagnose plant diseases in three critical crops using AI — even without internet access.

- 🌱 **Enset** — detects bacterial wilt, root rot, heart-leaf wilting
- 🌾 **Teff** — detects leaf rust, head smudge, stem lesions  
- 🌿 **Wheat** — detects stripe rust, stem rust, powdery mildew

---

## Features

- 🤖 **AI Diagnosis** — powered by Gemini AI with photo analysis
- 📱 **Offline Mode** — works without internet, syncs when reconnected
- 🗣️ **4 Languages** — English, Amharic (አማርኛ), Oromiffa, Tigrinya
- 📞 **Nearest Help** — direct call links to agricultural extension offices
- 🗃️ **History** — stores all past diagnoses on device
- 🎤 **Voice Input** — describe symptoms by speaking

---

## Why This Matters

No AI tool exists for Teff disease detection. Ethiopian farmers lose crops every season without access to expert advice. This tool puts a plant pathologist in every farmer's pocket — for free, in their language, offline.
