🌿 Resilience Guardian

An offline-first AI tool helping Ethiopian smallholder farmers diagnose and manage diseases in Enset, Teff, and Wheat — even without internet.

About the Project

Built to bridge the gap between modern AI and traditional Ethiopian farming knowledge — giving every farmer access to expert plant health advice in their language, on their phone, wherever they are.

Built by: Gulilat Kasiye Worku — Ethiopian AI Developer

Version History

  • v1.0 Basic UI + Crop Selection (May 2025)
  • v2.0 Photo upload + Symptom selector
  • v3.0 Gemini AI integration + Voice input
  • v4.0 Multi-language support (Amharic focus)
  • v5.0 Full PWA + Offline caching
